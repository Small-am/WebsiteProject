Biography/Summary of the Montoya Twins

Mya and Myka Montoya, twins born on September 12, 2000, in Clovis, New Mexico. Would spend their childhood there until, moving to Amarillo, Texas in 2009. As twins you'll never see them apart. First recognized, the Montoya twins accidentally created Musical.ly accounts, and there on out dramastically began to become popular for their trending, creative musical.ly videos in 2015 and their popularity began to rise even more. A year later, Myka & Mya created their first YouTube channel, Montoya Twins. For everyone, this was a new beginning of something big for the Montoya Twins. Then a year later, in September 2017, they announced they would move to Los Angeles, California, where you can see their rising popularity landed a Facebook Watch TV series where they blog about their rise, daily routines, and lifestyle as growing creators in the social media city of Los Angeles. In 2019, currently 19 years old, their fame has been on the rise ever since their musical.ly videos, as they continue to create amazing, creative youtube videos. We can see Mya & Myka grow into the fame easily with no problems and with great family support. With the support of their mother, Joanne Montoya, and father, Andres Montoya, they are the big key factors of assisting the girls in their success as big hit creators. With the support and increasing fan-base, There is no doubt that Mya & Myka in the future become big influential creators. There rising popularity has led them to already some great social media accolades and at this rate, will continue to achieve great success.

Facebook Watch Series:

Montoya'z Hollywood Break In, after their announcement of their plans to move to Los Angeles, California, they gained from Facebook a "Facebook watch series". Started October 2017, with currently 50 episodes and continuing. The series follows the Twins' transition from their small town of Amarillo, Texas to the big city of Los Angeles, California. Through their episodes, and almost 600k followers and growing, we watch as the Mya & Myka get exposure to the social media life in L.A and the many celebrities they meet along the way. As well as experience being in one of the largest cities in the U.S. As they are only 18 years old, the twins' path of success will continue to grow. On the other hand, we see the things and actions they do throughout their days, but also spend some quality time with their family as well. Meaning they never forget about what is important to them, family.
